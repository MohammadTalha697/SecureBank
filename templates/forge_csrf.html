What just happened?

When you clicked "Claim Voucher", this page silently submitted a hidden form to localhost:5000/transfer.

In Vulnerable Mode: The transfer goes through because there is no CSRF token check.

In Secure Mode: The server rejects it — the request has no valid csrf_token matching the session.

This is a real CSRF attack. The victim never sees the form. They just clicked a button on a fake page.
